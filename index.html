Short Biography

Weicheng Xie is currently an Assistant Professor in College of Computer Science and Software Engineering, Shenzhen University. He received the B.E. degree in Statistics from Central China Normal University in June 2008, and the Ph.D. degree in Computational Mathematics from Wuhan University in September 2013. Before joining Shenzhen University, he was a Visiting Scholar in the School of Computer Science, The University of Nottingham, UK, and a Postdoctor in the Computer Vision group with Prof. Linlin Shen, Shenzhen University. He is now heading the Facial Expression Analysis research group in Shenzhen University. He is a member of IEEE, CCF, and CAAI.
